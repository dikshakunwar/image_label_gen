About LabelGen.AI

Making image tagging intelligent, fast, and accessible through AI.

Our Mission

At LabelGen.AI, we aim to simplify the process of organizing and understanding visual content. By leveraging the power of Amazon Rekognition, we provide automated image labeling that saves time, reduces manual effort, and enhances image management at scale.

What We Do

LabelGen.AI enables users to upload images and receive AI-generated tags for objects, scenes, text, and more. Built on top of AWS Rekognition and integrated with Amazon S3 for secure cloud storage, our platform is both reliable and scalable for developers, creators, and businesses.

Why Choose Us?

  • Fast and accurate label detection
  • Secure image storage via AWS S3
  • Easy integration with Node.js backend
  • Scalable for teams and enterprises

Built With

Our platform is powered by:

  • AWS Rekognition
  • Amazon S3
  • Node.js
  • HTML/CSS/JavaScript frontend
